About Me

Hi, I am Ujjwal Shukla, a passionate and results driven Product Manager with over 3 years of experience across Product Development specializing in building and scaling API, AI, and SaaS driven products in both B2B SaaS and B2C ecosystems. My professional journey began with an MBA from IIT Kanpur and a B.Tech from IET Lucknow, equipping me with strong analytical and problem-solving skills. With over three years of experience in Product Management and Analytics, I bring a strategic mindset, data-driven decision-making, and hands-on execution to drive impactful products.
