About Me

Hi, I'm Ujjwal Kumar Shukla, a passionate and results-driven Product Manager with over 3.5 years of experience in the Product, Analytics, and Marketing industry.   • Growth Strategies: Developed and executed high-impact acquisition, engagement, and retention strategies across B2B and B2C markets.
  • User Engagement & Feedback: Conducted 150+ user interviews and feedback sessions to shape roadmap priorities and enhance user experience.
